# The Power of Awareness - Chapter 16

Self surrender is essential, and by that is meant ‘the confession of personal impotence. I can of mine own self do nothing.

Since creation is finished, it is impossible to force anything into being. The example of magnetism previously given is a good illustration. You cannot make magnetism; it can only be displayed. You cannot make [the law](/glossary#the-law) of magnetism. If you want to build a magnet, you can do so only by conforming to [the law](/glossary#the-law) of magnetism. In other words, you surrender yourself, or yield to the law. In like manner, when you use the faculty of [Assumption](/glossary#assumption), you are conforming to a law just as real as the law governing magnetism. You can neither create nor change [the law of Assumption](/lectures/radio-lectures/the-law-of-assumption). It is in this respect that you are impotent. You can only yield or conform, and since all of your experiences are the result of your assumptions (consciously or unconsciously) the value of consciously using the power of [Assumption](/glossary#assumption) surely must be obvious.

Willingly identify yourself with that which you most desire, knowing that it will find expression through you. Yield to the [feeling](/glossary#feeling) of the wish fulfilled and be consumed as its victim, then rise as the prophet of the [law of Assumption](/glossary#law-of-assumption).


## Summary
### Self Surrender is Essential

Confess personal impotence *"I can of myself do nothing."*

### Cannot Force Creation

Creation is finished, impossible to force anything into being.
Like magnetism, can only display and conform to its law.

### Surrender to the Law of Assumption

Using Assumption faculty conforms to its real law.
Cannot create or change the law, only yield and conform.

### Conscious Use Has Value

Experiences result from assumptions conscious/unconscious.
Conscious use of Assumption power has obvious value.

### Identify With and Yield to Desire

Willingly identify with what you desire, it will express.
Yield to wish fulfilled [feeling](/glossary#feeling) and be consumed by it.
Then rise as prophet of the law of Assumption.
